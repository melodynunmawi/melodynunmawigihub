Green papaya salad is a spicy salad made from shredded
unripe papaya. Originating from ethnic Lao people,
it is also eaten throughout Southeast Asia. Locally known
in Thailand as som tam, in Laos as tam som, or the more
specific name tam maak hoong, in Cambodia as bok
l'hong, and in Vietnam as gỏi đu đủ

A steak is a meat generally sliced across the muscle fibers,
potentially including a bone. It is normally grilled,
though can also be pan-fried. It is often grilled in
an attempt to replicate the flavor of steak cooked
over the glowing coals of an open fire.
